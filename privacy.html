Privacy Policy

Idasis provides a WhatsApp assistant for driver information. We use personal data only to operate the service, respond to user requests, maintain security, and comply with applicable law.

Information We Process

When you message Idasis on WhatsApp, we may process your WhatsApp phone number, profile name, message content, timestamps, command history, and technical logs. If you contact us by email, we process the information you send to us.

How We Use Information

We use information to reply to requests, provide route and road information, troubleshoot the assistant, prevent abuse, improve service quality, and answer support or privacy questions.

Marketing

Idasis does not sell personal data and does not send marketing broadcasts without consent. Users can stop communication at any time by messaging "stop" or by contacting us.

Service Providers

WhatsApp messages are processed through WhatsApp and Meta services. Website hosting, logging, or operational tools may process limited technical data needed to run the service.

Retention

We keep operational message and technical logs only as long as needed for support, security, and service reliability, unless a longer period is required by law.

Your Choices

You may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal data by emailing contact@idasis.eu.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Idasis at contact@idasis.eu.

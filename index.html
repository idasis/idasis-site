Service

Clear road information for drivers

Idasis is a WhatsApp assistant that helps users request practical driving information, route notes, and public travel updates in a simple message format.

The assistant is independent. Idasis is not a government agency, transport authority, carrier, insurer, repair service, or emergency roadside assistance provider.

Approach

Public information, simple answers

Idasis summarizes publicly available information and user-requested route context. Road conditions, rules, and service availability can change quickly, so users should verify critical details before travel.
